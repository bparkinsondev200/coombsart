About Me

Hi there! I’m a Utah-based motion graphics artist with experience in storyboarding and illustration. I studied animation and game design at Utah Valley University, where I was the director and lead game designer for both award-winning senior studio projects. I gained a great understanding of various project pipelines and project management. After graduating, I learned motion graphics in order to make myself more marketable in the Utah area, and have been employed as a motion graphics artist since then. To keep my skills sharp, I work on illustrating, animations and other motion graphics in my free time. I have experience in several different areas, have strong leadership skills, am adaptable and have a critical eye for the demands of the film and game industries. I’m always looking for further opportunities in this industry and look forward to making more connections. Thanks for visiting my site and learning more about me.
